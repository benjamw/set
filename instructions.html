Instructions

How to Play

When you find a set, click on the cards in that set, and they will be highlighted, and if the set is valid, they will be removed from the group and replaced with new cards from the deck.

If the set is invalid, an error will show and the cards will not be removed.

If there are no sets in the group, click on the 'No Sets' button and if you are correct, three more cards will be added to the group. If you are wrong, then an error will show and no cards will be added.

If there are more than 12 cards in the group and you find a set, no new cards will be added, instead, the remainder of the cards will be integrated into the main group until you have only 12 cards in the group.

Object of the game

The object of the game is to find sets in the group of cards that form a set by having all attributes different, or some of the attributes different, and some of them the same.

An attribute is one of: color, number, shape, fill
